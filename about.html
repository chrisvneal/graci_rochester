Mission Statement

Incredible Transformation's Power Couples of God's Mission is to strengthen relationships, marriages, and homes of all those who are seeking to build through Christ. Our overall mission is to raise awareness on abuse within the relationship and teach all couples that knowledge building starts at home. Incredible Transformation's Power Couples of God seeks to promote the real victory a couple will receive in the joy of walking with God and serving while practicing their unity.

Purpose

God's Kingdom started with unity and in that garden beautiful things happen between man and woman of which God created it.

Genesis 2:18

...I will make him a helper suitable for him.

New American Standard Bible

The sad thing is that many are not honoring this script. The primary purpose is to promote healthier relationships and the view of God. Under His radar, all things are possible. We will bring about unity, support one another in reaching our goals in our love, careers, business, and much more.

Too much disrespect and abuse continues among marriages and relationships, and cheating and adulterous behavior is becoming increasingly out of control. Men and women do not know where to place their lust and anger control issues. This association will help couples place themselves back in control of their emotions with coaching and a series of events, co-created with the dynamic tools that will be put in place.
